📸  HOW TO USE
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Go to Case Stories page
2. Click on any case thumbnail
3. Image opens full-screen with:
   - ◀ ▶ Navigation arrows
   - Image counter
   - Caption describing the photo
   - X button to close

4. Navigate:
   - Click arrows or use keyboard ← →
   - Press ESC to close
   - Click outside image to close
