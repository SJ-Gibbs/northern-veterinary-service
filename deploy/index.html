About Us

Northern Veterinary Service (NVS) brings specific veterinary expertise directly to patients across Northern England. Our multidisciplinary mobile service ensures that animals receive the highest quality surgical care in the comfort of their own veterinary practice.

We partner with veterinary practices to provide advanced surgical procedures, diagnostic imaging and general consultants, and ongoing support for complex cases.
