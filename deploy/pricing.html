Additional Costs to Consider

The prices listed above represent the surgeon's fee only. Your practice will also need to factor in:

  • Anaesthesia and monitoring
  • Surgical materials and consumables
  • Post-operative medications
  • Hospitalization and nursing care
  • Follow-up consultations

We're happy to provide detailed quotes including estimated material costs for your specific case.
